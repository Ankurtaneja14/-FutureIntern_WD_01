What is a domain name?

Simply put a domain name is the name of a website. It’s what comes after “www.” In a web address. If someone asks how to find you online, what you tell them is usually your domain name. Examples of domain names are: stellersites.info or stellarbsi.com

Can I choose my own domain name?

Of course! Depending on availability, you can choose any domain name you’d like. Domain names are limited to availability, similar to instagram usernames or twitter usernames.

What is web hosting?

Web hosting servers are the space where your website can live and be stored while being accessible on the internet. If you want your website to be live online – in other words, if you want anyone other than yourself to see your website- then web hosting is a necessary part of the equation. Without it, your website will be un accessible to the public.

Are there any ongoing fees once my site is built?

Yes. There is a hosting and service fee for all packages starting at $29 per month.
